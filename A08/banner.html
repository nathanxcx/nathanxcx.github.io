About Sky: Children of the Light

Sky is a serene and enchanting adventure game where players embark on a journey through a breathtaking, cloud-filled kingdom. As a Child of Light, you glide across stunning landscapes, discover hidden realms, and collaborate with other players to restore fallen stars to their constellations. The game emphasizes themes of connection, generosity, and exploration, offering a unique social experience through its wordless communication and cooperative puzzles, all set to a beautiful, evolving musical score.
